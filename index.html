Stocks Predictor

Collaboratively developed a system for predicting stock prices across multiple companies using LSTM-based deep learning models. The project includes fetching and preprocessing stock data, integrating market indices like the S&P 500, and generating time-series sequences. As a group, we worked on evaluating predictions against a random walk baseline and visualizing results through detailed plots. Automated workflows ensure efficient processing and reproducibility, utilizing libraries such as TensorFlow, Pandas, and Scikit-learn to streamline collaboration and execution.
